Who We Are

TBC Command Hub – The Core of Creative Management

TBC Command Hub is an advanced digital management and control dashboard operated by TBC Business Club, and owned by Turk Palace A.Ş, a company officially registered in the Republic of Türkiye.

The platform aims to empower entrepreneurs, university students, and trainees to develop their skills and businesses within a smart, practical, and modern environment.

We do not offer theoretical content only. Instead, we provide an integrated system for membership management, activity tracking, and performance development, based on smart education and behavioral impact, aligned with the demands of today’s market.

Our Mission
